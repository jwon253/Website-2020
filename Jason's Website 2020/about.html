Education

What High School Qualifications Do I Need?

At high school the recommended subjects for taking mechanical engineering is maths and science. For maths, calculus and trigonometry. For science, physics and chemistry. You will also need some understanding of algebra, geometry and trigonometry.

Employment

What Job Opportunities Are There?

There are many job opportunities for taking a bachelor's degree in mechanical engineering. Some examples include:

  • Mechanical Engineer
  • Aerospace Engineer
  • Automotive Engineer
  • Maintenance Engineer
  • Manufacturing Engineer
  • Robotics Engineer

What Other Related Jobs Could I Do?

Mechanical Engineering can branch of many other related engineering jobs. Some examples include:

  • Electrical Engineer
  • Electronics Engineer
  • Biomedical Engineer
  • Aeronautical Engineer
  • Material engineer

Will Robots Take Over My Job?

Mechanical Engineers will not be replaced by robots. As this job is ranked #53 out of #702, means the job is less likely to be replaced. Not any time soon as mechanical engineers are the people who create automation technology. But who knows what jobs are for the future.
