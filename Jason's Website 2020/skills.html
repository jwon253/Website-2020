What Personal Qualities Do I Need?

Mechanical engineering requires many different qualities, some qualities are.

  • Critical thinking
  • Compassion
  • Curiosity
  • Resilient
  • Integrity
  • Attention to detail

Creativity

Mechanical engineers need the ability to innovate, develop and design new products, find alternative ways to create new solutions to solve problem.

Cooperation

Mechanical engineers need the ability to communicate with others and collaborate. Together they could develop something extraordinary.

Problem Solving

An essential ability that all mechanical engineers require is problem solving. They use this skill to solve issues, analyse small error and identify improvements.

What Skills & Knowlodge Do I Need?

Mechanical engineers uses a combinations of both soft and hard skills. They use many different skills but the main skills needed for engineering is problem solving, creativity and cooperation. Mechanical engineers required to have mathematics skills to solve problmes, calculations. Also knowledge of physics, electronics, thermodynamics and safety regulations.
